### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working and Learning Environment**  
The working environment is well-organized with a clear weekly roadmap. FCJ members are always friendly, open, and willing to help even outside working hours. The combination of theoretical learning, Hands-on Labs, and project implementation made the experience highly practical. However, I think it would be nice to have more social gatherings or team bonding activities to strengthen team relationships.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provided detailed guidance and constantly encouraged me to ask questions. The admin team effectively supported all administrative tasks. What I appreciate most is that instead of just giving me the direct answer, the mentor guided me to read official AWS documentation and solve problems independently. This approach significantly improved my debugging and analytical skills.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned aligned perfectly with my Software Engineering major. University knowledge regarding RESTful APIs, database management, and system architecture was directly applied to build the *Smart Home Energy Waste Monitoring* project. Moreover, the internship introduced me to modern cloud computing areas that are not deeply covered in my university curriculum.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the 12 weeks, I learned numerous new skills, ranging from project management and teamwork to professional communication. Technically, I gained hands-on experience with AWS Lambda, DynamoDB, API Gateway, S3, CloudWatch, etc. I also significantly improved my technical documentation skills and system design mindset based on the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone works seriously but maintains an enjoyable atmosphere. Working on a comprehensive project helped me realize how different AWS services integrate together, rather than learning them in isolation. Participating in the FCAJ Community Day also gave me a great sense of belonging in the broader Cloud community.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The flexible working hours and the internship allowance were great benefits. Additionally, having access to structured Hands-on Labs and internal training sessions is a massive advantage for any intern.

### Additional Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  The most valuable experience was building a complete cloud-native project from scratch, including deploying, testing, and writing documentation. It allowed me to see the "big picture" of how a real-world system operates.

- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
  I hope the program can provide additional advanced workshops focusing on CI/CD, Infrastructure as Code (Terraform/CloudFormation), and real-world enterprise architecture deployment scenarios.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
  Absolutely yes. I would highly recommend this internship to anyone passionate about AWS because it provides a structured roadmap, practical challenges, and excellent opportunities to hone problem-solving skills.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
  It would be great if the program organized more technical sharing sessions featuring experienced AWS professionals to provide deeper industry insights.
- **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
  If given the opportunity, I would love to participate in advanced training programs regarding Cloud Architecture, DevOps, or Artificial Intelligence on AWS.
- **Any other comments (free sharing):**  
  This internship has been a meaningful milestone in my learning journey. A sincere thank you to the Mentor and the FCJ team for their support and for providing a solid foundation for my future career.
